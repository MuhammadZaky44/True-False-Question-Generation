Synonym/Antonym Substitution Template

Synonym/Antonym substitution template will find any word in the sentence and replace that word with their synonym/antonym in the dictionary. The dictionary is based on Indonesian thesaurus synonym/antonym.

Synonym will replace up to 2 words randomly, while antonym will only replace a word randomly in the sentence

If there is any changes in the sentence, the changes will be marked with colorblue for synonym andred for antonym.
